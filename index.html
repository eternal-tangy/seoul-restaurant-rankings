How It Works

  • 1

    Pick a neighbourhood & category

    e.g. Top 5 Sushi in Dohwa-dong, Mapo-gu, Seoul

  • 2

    Analyse real Seoul data

    Foot traffic, card payment trends, and commercial density are pulled from Seoul Open Data Portal.

  • 3

    Score & rank

    A composite score ranks restaurants — foot traffic 40%, card payment 40%, commercial density 20%.

  • 4

    Publish a video guide

    A script is generated and filmed — giving foreign visitors honest, data-backed recommendations.
